Posts

Posts are the heart of reddit content. Here are some statistics about them.
Some posts, termed "self posts", contain only text content. Everything else carries some sort link to content like an image, video, or external website.
About 41% of posts are text only.

Karma

Users on reddit rate posts by giving "upvotes" to ones they like; "downvotes" to ones they don't. The difference between the two gives a post its score and awards the poster "karma". The average score of a post is just over 68, but this is heavily skewed by a small number of very high scoring posts. Most posts receive almost no upvotes or downvotes.
The average score of posts varies over the course of a day (times are in PST).
And also slightly by day of week.
